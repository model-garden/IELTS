## 一、核心答题结构（今天只用这一套）

### 1️⃣ Yes 型（主力，约 70–80% 题目）

```text
Yes, I do.
Because + 原因，and + 轻微延展 / 细节
```

**示例**
```text
Yes, I do.
Because it helps me relax after a busy day, and I usually do it in the evening.
```

---

### 2️⃣ No 型（差异化加分）

```text
Not really / Not exactly.
Because + 原因，so + 替代行为
```

**示例**
```text
Not really.
Because it feels time-consuming for me, so I usually choose simpler options.
```

---

### 3️⃣ Depends 型（7 分意识，必会）

```text
It depends.
If 情况 A, + 选择 A
But if 情况 B, + 选择 B
```

**示例**
```text
It depends.
If I need to focus, I prefer working alone, but for teamwork projects, I enjoy working with others.
```

## 二、Day 2 高频 Part 1 题库（20 题）

> **使用方法**：
> - 每题限时 **20 秒**
> - 至少 **2 句**
> - 必须使用 Yes / No / Depends 开头

### 🟢 Topic 1：Daily Life
1. Do you usually get up early?

   Yes, I do.

   I need to be at work before 9 o'clock, so I usually get up around 7 to have breakfast and take the subway.

2. Do you like staying at home?

   It depends.

   If the weather is sunny, I prefer to go outside.

   But if it's rainny, I usually stay at home.

3. Do you often use public transport?

   Yes, I do.

   Because public transport is cheap and convenient, and I don't have a driving licence anyway.

4. Do you plan things in advance?

   It depends.

   If it's important, I like to plan ahead,

   but if it's something simple, I usually don't worry too much about it.

5. Do you like your daily routine?

   It depends.

   If my daily routine is too busy or stressful, I don't really enjoy it,

   but if it's comfortable and easy, I'm quite happy with it.

### 🟢 Topic 2：Study / Work
6. Are you working or studying now?
7. Do you enjoy your major or job?
8. Do you prefer studying alone or with others?
9. Is your work or study stressful?
10. Do you often use English at work or school?

### 🟢 Topic 3：Leisure
11. Do you like watching movies?
12. Do you listen to music every day?
13. Do you like travelling?
14. Do you prefer weekends or weekdays?
15. Do you like sports?

### 🟢 Topic 4：Technology / Habits
16. Do you often use social media?
17. Do you prefer texting or calling?
18. Do you use your phone a lot?
19. Do you like online shopping?
20. Do you often watch videos online?

## 三、模板使用比例建议（高投入产出比）

| 类型 | 建议数量 | 说明 |
|----|----|----|
| Yes | 14–15 题 | 主体答案，最省力 |
| No | 3–4 题 | 拉开与背模板考生差距 |

## 四、跟读训练（10 分钟）

**材料建议**：
- YouGlish（搜索：`Yes, I do because`）
- IELTS Liz / IELTS Simon Part 1 音频

**方法**：

1. 一句一句暂停
2. 模仿语调而非速度
3. 同一句读 3 遍
4. 不查生词，不背词
